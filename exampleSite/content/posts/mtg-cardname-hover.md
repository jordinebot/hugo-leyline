The `cardname` shortcode keeps an article flowing while still giving readers a quick card reference on hover.

The classic {{< cardname "Brainstorm" >}} sees play in cube, legacy, and pauper alike.

```
The classic {{</* cardname "Brainstorm" */>}} sees play in cube, legacy, and pauper alike.
```
