Welcome to Leyline

What Leyline is, what it isn't, and the design choices behind it.

Leyline is a minimal Hugo theme aimed at writing. It does a few things well:

  • Sticky header with a logo, menu, and quick controls.
  • A light/dark switcher that respects your OS preference until you choose otherwise.
  • A quiet home page that surfaces featured posts (or your latest, if you haven’t picked any).

Why “Leyline”?

The name nods to invisible threads of structure — the kind of design you don’t notice because everything is where you’d expect.

What’s next

  • Wire up real search.
  • Add a couple of utility shortcodes.
  • Ship a darker, denser variant for code-heavy blogs.
